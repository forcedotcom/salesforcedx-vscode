| Action | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Clear All Results** (Test Explorer `...` menu) | Removes pass/fail icons from the tree. Result files remain on disk and will be restored on next refresh or reload. |
| **SFDX: Clear Apex Test Results** (Command Palette) | Removes pass/fail icons **and** deletes stored result files. Results will not reappear on refresh or reload. |
